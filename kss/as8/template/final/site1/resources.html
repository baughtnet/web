What is the internet?

The internet is a vast network of interconnected computers that allows people from all over the world to share information, communicate, and access a wide range of resources. It has become an integral part of our daily lives, influencing how we work, learn, and socialize. At its core, the internet functions like a massive library that contains almost every piece of information you can imagine. It's like having billions of books at your fingertips, covering every topic under the sun. Whether you are researching a school project, learning a new skill, or simply looking for the latest news, the internet provides an abundance of information. - The internet allows us to communicate and connect with people from every corner of the globe. Just like sending letters or talking on the phone, we can now use various platforms like email, messaging apps, and social media to stay in touch with friends, family, and even strangers who share our interests. - It provides a wealth of entertainment options. With streaming services, online gaming, and platforms like YouTube, there is an endless array of movies, TV shows, music, and videos available at any time. It's like having a giant virtual entertainment center that never closes. - The internet also serves as a platform for self-expression. People can create personal websites, blogs, or YouTube channels to share their thoughts, ideas, and creativity. It's a venue to showcase your talents or express yourself in ways that were not possible before. - In terms of comparison, you can think of the internet as a vast digital city where individuals and organizations set up virtual shops, libraries, museums, and gathering places. It's a bustling metropolis where you can explore, learn, and engage with others. - It's important to note that while the internet offers incredible opportunities, it's essential to use it responsibly. Being mindful of your digital footprint, protecting your personal information, and being cautious about online interactions are essential aspects of internet safety. In summary, the internet revolutionizes the way we access information, communicate, and entertain ourselves. It serves as a global library, connecting people worldwide, and providing platforms for creativity and self-expression. It's like a virtual city that never sleeps, offering endless possibilities and knowledge. Nonetheless, staying safe and responsible online is just as important as enjoying the vast benefits the internet has to offer.

What is coding?

Coding or programming simply put is giving the computer instructions in a structured format to have it accomplish a specific task. Coding is embedded in all kinds of everyday devices including the webpage you are looking at right now, your appliances at home or even in the diesel motors running the school buses.
