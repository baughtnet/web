Destination Spotlight

Ditch the ordinary vacation and dive into adventure! Cruise ships are floating playgrounds packed with endless activities, new experiences, and opportunities to connect with friends. Explore exotic destinations, soak up the sun, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Quick Facts:

  • Amazing and unlimited food: Coffee Bars, Pizza Stands, Ice Cream and so much more!
  • Popular destinations: Caribbean, Bahamas, Mediterranean
  • Onboard: Pools, waterslides, shows, movies, parties, rock climbing
  • Shore excursions: Island hopping, snorkeling, ziplining, historical tours
