The Best Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter (or chunky if preferred)
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in peanut butter, egg, and vanilla extract until combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Roll dough into 1-inch balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Flatten the balls with a fork and bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Pro Tips

  • Bake until almost done, the edges should just start to color. They will continue to set after being removed from the oven.
  • Measure flour correctly! Too much flour makes dense cookies. Use a scale or fluff the flour and level it off.
  • Cookies are soft when warm, let them cool before transferring. They will firm up as they cool.
  • Store in an airtight container for up to a week.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why fork marks? They help the cookies spread evenly and bake correctly.
  • Perfect fork marks? Use skewers instead of a fork for a clear criss-cross pattern.
  • Best peanut butter? Smooth, salted is ideal. Unsweetened works too, adjust sugar.
  • Chill the dough? Not necessary, but chilling is okay.
